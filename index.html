About

Our Story

G.T. Trucking Services started as a subcontracted company for an affiliate transport company in 2022. The company was officially founded and registered as a legitimate trucking business in August 2023. Since then, it has continued to expand due to the increasing demand for trucking and hauling services in the Philippines.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the top choice for trucking services nationwide through our dedication to providing efficient, reliable, and convenient hauling and transportation services. In the future, we aim to expand as a global logistics brokerage company.

Our Mission

Our mission is to consistently provide top-notch and high-quality solutions to business entities that require trucking services. We are committed to continuous innovation to improve and enhance our services.

Our Services

Containerized Import/Export

Full truckload transportation

Hustling and loose cargo

Refrigerated trucking services

Flatbed and oversized hauling

Why Choose Us?

As a startup company, we have grown rapidly and will continuously grow with momentum behind with the help of expirienced drivers and helpers, some of which have 20 years of expirience in the industry.

Brief History

Our trucking started in November 2022 under subcontract with an affiliate transport company, we registered our trucking company last August 2023, and just started fully operating in 2023 as a Bonafide Trucking Company with global clients like Hankyu Hanshin Express Philippines, "K"Line Logistics(Phils.)INC., TSL Freight Movers INC., Emo Trans Philippines INC., Alpha one and TBB Universal Logistics INC. We would like to offer the same service that we offer to your company as part of our plan to expand this year with new partners like you. We will aim to fulfill your company's needs the same way that we do with our current clients.

Hustling Services

Our company also offers husting services from empty container to actual acceptance and dismounting of loaded container 24/7 from ground to container yard(CY) and process of transferring the container from warehouse to loading area.

All available units are PEZA registered

Equipment

We currently have two (2) units available.

  • 2 – 6W Tractor Head
  • 2 – 40ft Combo Chassis
